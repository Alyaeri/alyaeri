Project details

Enquire is a revolutionary Grant Management system launching to global success, working in partnership with governments all over the world to provide a reliable system for the distribution of government funds to community improvement projects all over their country.

The Management Portal is the center of the Enquire system. It's where users create and maintain projects, process payments, track tasks and trigger workflows. This is the portal that ensures everything goes as smoothly as possible within the project. It is highly complex, incredibly efficient, and exceptionally secure. The Management Portal underwent many UI changes as the requirements developed, until we came to its latest design. Users have reported that this UI is significantly easier to follow than what they have used in the past, and has simplified their working process dramatically. For such a complex system, it was incredibly rewarding to hear users refer to it as 'intuitive' and 'simple'.
