Project details

The 'Interaction Exporation' project is a self improvement project that aims to sharpen my skills in the wireframe, mockups, and prototyping area, with regards to interaction and motion. These low fidelity interaction concepts were developed in Atomic.io, a revolutionary new prototyping tool that has made exploring motion between elements much more accessible for designers.

I have found that using a tool such Atomic, Adobe Illustrator, or a simple pen on paper, has increased my productivity tremendously. They facilitate free thinking and problem solving, with the 'user experience first' goal in mind, increasing user understand within my designs.
