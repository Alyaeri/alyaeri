Enquire Web App


Project Details

Enquire is a revolutionary Grant Management system launching to global success, working in partnership with governments all over the world to provide a reliable system for the distribution of government funds to community improvement projects all over their country.

Overview

The Enquire Web App is split into three distinct portals, Management, Client, and Admin. The Management Portal is for the powerhouse, where users spend most of their time in the workflow processes.The Client Portal is for external users to apply for grants towards their community projects, as well as update the status of their outstanding projects. The Admin Console provides the flexibility to conigure the others apps to the clients liking.

Management Portal details

The Management Portal is the center of the Enquire system. It's where users create and maintain projects, process payments, track tasks and trigger workflows. This is the portal that ensures everything goes as smoothly as possible within the project. It is highly complex, incredibly efficient, and exceptionally secure. The Management Portal underwent many UI changes as the requirements developed, until we came to its latest design. Users have reported that this UI is significantly easier to follow than what they have used in the past, and has simplified their working process dramatically. For such a complex system, it was incredibly rewarding to hear users refer to it as 'intuitive' and 'simple'.

Client Portal details

The Client Portal is the public facing webapp that allows users to apply for grants and funding from the respective Enquire client. This portal steps the users through the process of applying for funding, and interfaces directly with the Management Portal for the applications to be processed by government workers. Subject to approval, the public users then maintain their project through the Client Portal, keeping the funding provider informed about the projects process, and any changes to their original applications information.

Considering this webapp is public facing, and will be utilised by a significantly larger group of people that the Management Portal or Administration Console, it was imperative that the Client Portal be as user friendly as possible, and the results from users have spoken for themselves.

Admin Console details

The Administration Console is the backbone of the Enquire System. It allows each client to configure their own Enquire Instance to suit their companies needs. There is no one solution to grant management, and the Administration Console allows clients to maintain their own data, update their own forms, and modify Enquire extensively, to suit their needs. As their needs change and grow, so does Enquire.
