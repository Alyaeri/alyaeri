Bio

Hey! I'm Rachelle Patman - a dedicated User Interface + User Experience Designer based in Brisbane, Australia.

I have over 5 years experience in the design space, working with everything from bespoke Webapps, to company branding and print media. All of this experience is backed by a Bachelors in Multimedia Design from The University of Queensland, as well as a Certificate II in Information Technology.

I'm currently working as lead UI/UX Designer with Oversi. Previously, I worked as Lead UI/UX Designer with Tactiv on their Enquire suite.
